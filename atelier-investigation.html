Google Dorking

Opérateurs

  • "expression exacte"
  • OR ⇨ ce terme OU l'autre terme : Google OR vie privée
  • - ⇨ mot à exclure de la recherche : Google -privacy
  • * ⇨ caractère joker : rock * roll
  • 10..20 ⇨ échelle : Sarkozy 2002..2003
  • in ⇨ conversion : 250 $ in €

Targets

  • intitle:
    search something in the title of the page
  • site:mysite.com
    search something only on mysite.com 
  • inurl:
    search something in the url of a page
  • intext: search something inthe page body
  • filetype:pdf search a specific file type
  • link:http://site.com
    search every pages linking to site.com

Combinations

"Macron" intitle:"top 5..10 facts" -site:youtube.com inurl:2015

Juicy stuffs

intitle:”curriculum vitae” “Tel * * *” “adresse *” “e-mail”

Find copied pasted text

intext:"ma phrase pompée ailleurs" -site:monsite.com

Find other TLD

site:amazon.* -site:amazon.com
